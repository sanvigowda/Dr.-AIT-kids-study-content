# Visual Narrative Techniques

Data storytelling is about moving from "What happened" to "So what" and "Now what".

## 1. The Narrative Arc
- **Context**: The problem/scenario.
- **Climax**: The insight/finding.
- **Resolution**: The recommended action.

## 2. Design Principles
- **Signal vs Noise**: Remove everything that doesn't add value.
- **Color with Purpose**: Use color to highlight the insight, not just for decoration.
- **Annotation**: Use text to explain the "So what" directly on the chart.
