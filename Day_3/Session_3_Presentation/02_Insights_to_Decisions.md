# Turning Insights into Decisions

The final step of data analytics is driving change.

## 1. Actionable Insights
An insight is only useful if it's actionable.
- **Bad**: "Sales are down in Chennai."
- **Good**: "Sales are down 15% in Chennai due to product stockouts. Urgent restock of category X is required."

## 2. Presenting to Stakeholders
- **Exec Summary**: Bottom line upfront.
- **Evidence**: The data backing the claim.
- **The Ask**: What do you want them to do?
