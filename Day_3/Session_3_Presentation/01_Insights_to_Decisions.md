# Turning Insights into Decisions

> **Learning Goal:** Learn how to communicate your data findings in a way that leads to real-world business actions.

---

## Data vs. Insight vs. Decision

Analysts are not paid to produce "Data." They are paid to produce **Decisions**.

- **Data**: "Sales in Store C were 10 Lakhs last month." (A fact)
- **Insight**: "Store C had 20% lower footfall but 15% higher AOV than average." (A discovery)
- **Decision**: "Launch a loyalty program in Store C to increase visit frequency of high-value shoppers." (An action)

---

## The Pyramid Principle (Minto Pyramid)

Start with the **Conclusion**.

1. **Conclusion**: We should expand our 'Sports' category in Mumbai.
2. **Key Arguments**:
    - Sports revenue grew 40% MoM in Mumbai.
    - Mumbai customers have the highest AOV for Sports equipment.
3. **Supporting Data**: Charts showing category growth and location-wise AOV.

---

## Industry Tips

- **Quantify the impact**: Instead of "Sales will go up," say "This change is expected to increase revenue by 5 Lakhs per quarter."
- **Identify the owner**: Who needs to act on this?
- **Keep it Simple**: If you can't explain your insight in 30 seconds, you don't understand it well enough yet.
