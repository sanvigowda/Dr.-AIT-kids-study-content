#  Customer Insights & Segmentation

> **Learning Goal:** Learn how to group customers into segments to provide personalized experiences.

---

## 1 Behavioral Segmentation

Dividing customers based on their actions.
- **Member vs. Non-Member**: Premium members usually have higher AOV.
- **Payment Method**: Do UPI users buy more frequently?

---

## 2 RFM Analysis

- **Recency**: How recently did they buy?
- **Frequency**: How often do they buy?
- **Monetary**: How much total have they spent?

---

##  Industry Case: The "Dormant Member"

Through data, you find a group of customers who:
1. Are **Premium Members**.
2. Have **Recency** of > 90 days.

**Insight**: These are your VIPs who are about to churn.
**Action**: Send them a "We Miss You" coupon.
