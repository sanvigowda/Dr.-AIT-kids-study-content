#  KPI Identification in Retail

> **Learning Goal:** Learn how to translate raw data into "Key Performance Indicators" (KPIs) that business leaders care about.

---

##  Top Retail KPIs

### 1. Total Revenue (Sales)
- **Formula**: `SUM(Amount)`
- **Goal**: High growth over time.

### 2. Average Order Value (AOV)
- **What it is**: The average amount spent by a customer per visit.
- **Formula**: `Total Revenue / Total Transaction Count`

### 3. Basket Size (Units per Purchase)
- **Formula**: `Total Items Sold / Total Transaction Count`

---

##  Industry context: The "Why" behind KPIs

Imagine Store A has high **Revenue** but low **AOV**.
- **Insight**: Many people visit, but buy small/cheap items.
- **Action**: Launch "Buy 2 Get 1" offers or bundle high-value products.
