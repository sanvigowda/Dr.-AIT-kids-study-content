# KPI Identification & Definition

Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) are the metrics that matter most for a business.

## Common Retail KPIs
- **Total Revenue**: Sum of all sales.
- **Average Order Value (AOV)**: Revenue divided by number of orders.
- **Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)**: Cost to get one new customer.
- **Churn Rate**: Percentage of customers who stop using a service.

## Strategy
Always align KPIs with the business goal. If the goal is "Growth", track "New Customer Growth %".
