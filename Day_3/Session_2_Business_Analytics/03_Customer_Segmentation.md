# Customer Segmentation

Dividing a customer base into groups of individuals that are similar in specific ways.

## RFM Analysis
One of the most powerful segmentation techniques:
- **Recency**: How recently did the customer buy?
- **Frequency**: How often do they buy?
- **Monetary**: How much do they spend?

## Segments Examples
- **Champions**: High R, High F, High M.
- **At Risk**: Low R, High F, High M.
- **New Customers**: High R, Low F, Low M.
